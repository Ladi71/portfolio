Hey, I’m Ladina Wasescha

I’m 18 years old and I’m from Switzerland. I’m currently pursuing my graphic design education at the Fachklasse Grafik in Lucerne, now in my 5th semester. I’m especially interested in design that is built on an intriguing and well-thought-out concept. My main interests lie in concept development as well as editorial, poster, and animation projects. However, I’m passionate about everything related to graphic design, so my interests are quite broad. I’m an open-minded, curious, and motivated person. My goal is to gain lots of experience and to deepen and broaden my interests!
