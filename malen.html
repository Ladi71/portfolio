From Strokes to Painting
2023 ⋅ Painting

The smell of paint and the act of close observation accompanied me throughout this project. Studies of various objects – such as stones, vegetables, and canning jars – as well as capturing moods and different times of day kept me engaged. Through brushstrokes that were unpractised at first, I gradually developed joy and confidence in painting over the course of the project. Later, I was able to apply the insights I gained to further projects.
