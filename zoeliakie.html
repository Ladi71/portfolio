Glutenfree-Diet is Not just a “Trend”!
2025 ⋅ Poster ⋅ Animation ⋅ Goldbach Neo

As a continuation of the photography project, I explored the topic of coeliac disease in greater depth. Many people are still unfamiliar with this condition. The aim of this poster is to raise awareness and provide information. Even though only a few are affected, their experiences should be heard and better understood. Eating gluten-free is not a trend; for those affected, it is a necessity, as consuming gluten can make them ill. This project serves as an educational poster, explaining symptoms, facts, and prevalence.
