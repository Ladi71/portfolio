From the Cassette to the Typeface
2024 ⋅ Font Design

The visual inventories of a cassette led, through deconstruction, to the design of a typeface inspired by the music of a synth-pop rock band. It is suitable for striking, poster-like applications and was originally created for an album cover. The typeface is characterised by bold, angular, and high-contrast shapes.
