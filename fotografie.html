The Cost of a Gluten-Free Diet
2025 ⋅ Photography

People who need to follow a gluten-free diet often rely on substitute products. In this photo series, these substitutes are placed alongside their gluten-containing counterparts to reveal differences in texture, form, and price. The extreme price difference raises the question of whether paying significantly more – for often less enjoyment – can truly be justified. I explore these comparisons mainly through various baked goods, using high-contrast photography and clear composition.
